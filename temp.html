How to play

  

    🏰 Goal: protect the keep and survive as long as you can.
    Build by day, fight by night.
  

  

    

      
🔨 Build

      
Pick a tool from the bottom bar, then click a tile to place.
        Press R or scroll the mouse wheel to rotate the ghost before placing.

    

    

      
💰 Collect & Farm

      
Click a building when 💰 floats over it. On farms, click plot to plant
        and click ripe crops to harvest. Open chests for instant gold.

    

    

      
🔧 Repair Damaged Buildings

      
Walls, houses, towers and other constructions go dark with cracks
        when their HP drops below 50%. Click a damaged building to repair it
        — it costs a small amount of gold based on how broken it is, restores
        full HP, and clears the cracks. Farms and animals aren't repaired this
        way. Tip: keep your gates and walls patched before the next night!

    

    

      
⚔️ Commands (RTS)

      
Left-click a unit (or drag-frame several) to select.
        Right-click any tile to send them there.

    

    

      
🎥 Camera

      
W A S D to pan ·
        middle-drag to rotate · mouse-wheel to zoom.

    

    

      
⌨️ Keys

      
1–9 tools · R rotate ·
        X demolish · Esc cancel / pause

    

  

  

    

      
🔨 Build

      
Tap a tool in the bottom bar, then tap a tile to place.
        The tool auto-deselects after each placement.

    

    

      
↻ Rotate

      
When a tool is selected a yellow ↻ button appears
        on the right edge — tap it to rotate the ghost 90° before placing.
        Same technique used by SimCity BuildIt & Township.

    

    

      
💰 Collect & Farm

      
Tap a building when 💰 floats over it. Tap a farm to plant /
        harvest. Tap a chest for instant gold.

    

    

      
🔧 Repair

      
Walls and towers go dark + cracked below 50% HP.
        Tap to repair — pays a small gold cost and restores full HP.

    

    

      
⚔️ Commands

      
Tap a unit to select it, then tap a tile to send it there.

    

    

      
🎥 Camera

      
One-finger drag pans like Google Maps — the spot
        under your finger stays under your finger. Two-finger pinch zooms,
        two-finger twist rotates, two-finger vertical drag tilts.
        Lost? Tap 👁 in the top-right to recenter on your keep.

    

    

      
🔘 Buttons

      
🔨 demolish · ⏸ pause · 👁 recenter · 🔄 restart · 🔊 sound · ↻ rotate (during build).
